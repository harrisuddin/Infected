Infected



Instructions


To move your player, use the arrow keys. To show the score table press and hold S. The aim of the game is to survive as long as possible. As the Humans, you will try to run from the infected players. The longer you survive, the more score you will earn. You will score one point for every second you survive of the 2 minute round. As the infected, your job is to infect the human players. The more players you infect, the more score you will earn. You will score 5 points for every human you infect.
